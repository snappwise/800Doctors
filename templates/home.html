About Us

Exceptional Care,
Anytime, Anywhere

Welcome to 800Doctor, Dubai’s premier provider of doctor-on-call and home healthcare services. Established in 2007, we specialize in delivering high-quality, personalized medical care right at your doorstep.

Our team of fully licensed practitioners is trained in various specialties, including family medicine, general surgery, paediatrics, physiotherapy, nursing, obstetrics, and gynaecology. We are available 24/7, 365 days a year, ensuring that you receive friendly and prompt healthcare whenever you need it.

At 800Doctor, we offer a comprehensive range of home healthcare services, including home nursing, IV therapy, blood tests, physiotherapy, and occupational therapy. Whether at your home, office, or hotel, we prioritize your comfort and convenience.
