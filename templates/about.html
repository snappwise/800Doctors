About Us

Your Health, Our Commitment:
A Journey Since 2007

At 800Doctor, we have been transforming healthcare in Dubai since 2007. As a premier provider of doctor-on-call and home healthcare services, we recognized the need for convenient, accessible medical care that fits the busy lifestyles of residents in the UAE.

With a passion for enhancing patient experiences, we pioneered the concept of in-home medical services, building a dedicated team of licensed healthcare professionals committed to delivering high-quality care at your doorstep. Today, our extensive range of services ensures that individuals can receive expert medical attention wherever they are, whether at home, in the office, or traveling.

Our Misision

Transforming Patient
Care Delivery

Our mission is to provide exceptional healthcare that meets the needs of every individual, particularly those who cannot access traditional medical facilities. We aim to deliver reliable, compassionate, and affordable medical services directly to our patients, ensuring their health and well-being are our top priorities.

Our Vision

Optimal 24 hours
Health Care

We envision a healthcare system where quality medical care is always accessible. Our goal is to revolutionize home healthcare by providing comprehensive, round-the-clock services that cater to the unique needs of each patient. We strive to reduce non-emergency visits to hospitals by offering prompt, professional consultations in the comfort of your home. Our commitment is to expand our exceptional services throughout Dubai and beyond, ensuring everyone can benefit from top-tier healthcare.
